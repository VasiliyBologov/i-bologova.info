Artist Irina Bologova

I live in Kaluga, Russia, I have a small painting studio in a country house.

I mostly paint landscapes and still lifes, I focus on impressions.

I usually use acrylics, oils or watercolors in various techniques. Sometimes I carry out orders for photomontage.

I mostly draw inspiration for my portraits from photographs. Landscapes and still lifes in my works are what surrounds me.

Nature, ordinary things in the house, flowers. I have a series of paintings with horses - this is more an exception. There are also many ideas coming from the Internet.

Participant of many exhibitions at the local and regional level.
